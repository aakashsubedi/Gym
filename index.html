ABOUT POWERFIT

Founded in 2010, PowerFit has grown to become the premier fitness destination for those serious about transforming their bodies and lives. Our state-of-the-art facilities span over 25,000 square feet of training space equipped with the latest fitness technology.

What sets us apart is our community of dedicated fitness professionals and members who create an atmosphere of motivation and support. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, we provide the environment and expertise to help you achieve your goals.
